Emalia Hinden-Stevenson


Hello! My name is Emalia. I was raised on Vashon Island, Washington, where I discovered a passion for digital media at a young age. I grew up homeschooled, “unschooling”, which allowed me lots of extra time to fully pursue filmmaking, culminating in a film called Lieutenant Lilly. Inspired by the experience of expressing the way I saw the world, I attended Hofstra University. I found a particular interest in the study of film theory and the psychological explanations for the ways media influences our thoughts and emotions, and have enjoyed a career in marketing since graduation. My love of my Jewish heritage, my feminist principles, and my lifelong outdoor sportswomanship have guided me and will continue to shape my path in life.
